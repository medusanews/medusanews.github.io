--- layout: page title: "Symptoms" date: 2013-10-01 22:42 keywords: symptoms description: Articles about symptoms sharing: true footer: true --- Sore throat
Acute inflammation of the throat causing hoarseness and a sharp discomfort when swallowing. In some cases, sore throat precedes the other symptoms of a viral or bacterial infection of the upper respiratory tract.

Pain in the chest
The feeling of pain or pressure in the chest can be a sign of how little problems such as indigestion, and much more serious, such as angina or myocardial infarction. In any case, you should be attentive to the emergence of such pain, especially those related to physical exertion or stress situations.

Pain in the abdomen
Abdominal pain ranges from dull to sharp or burning and shooting and arise in different diseases and conditions. If you experience severe pain or tenderness in the right lower abdomen, abdominal pain when walking, nausea, vomiting or loss of appetite, fever, you should immediately consult a doctor.

Diarrhea
Diarrhea - is loose stools, which can be caused by various reasons. Diarrhea, often accompanied by abdominal cramps, vomiting, or fever. In adults, diarrhea is rarely dangerous. The children of different ages and diarrhea in the elderly can cause dehydration, which may lead to serious consequences.

Constipation
Constipation - is hindered, delayed or insufficiently frequent defecation (bowel movement). In some cases, constipation may be a sign of a more serious disease. For example, alternating constipation and diarrhea may mean that you have irritable bowel syndrome (common disorder of the intestines). Diverticulitis, inflammation diverticulum - saccular structures in small bowel wall, frequently causes constipation, fever and pain in the lower left part of the abdomen.

Skin rash in children
Skin rash in children may be a manifestation of more than one hundred different diseases. You do not necessarily understand this set of states. However, some of them can be really dangerous for the baby. Therefore, the appearance of any rash should be in time to address the pediatrician. Do not forget that describe the appearance of the skin on the phone pointless, a doctor should see the changes with your own eyes.

Fever
Fever / heat - this increase in body temperature above 37 degrees (when measuring the temperature in the axilla or mouth). The body temperature of a person usually rises when the body is fighting off bacterial, viral, or parasitic infection, but it can also be connected to other, non-infectious problems: injury, heat stroke, dehydration, and worsening of some chronic diseases.

Unpleasant body odor
Each person has their own smell. However, when the human body odor is too strong or abrupt starts and repel others, it can be a serious problem. Unpleasant body odor is usually easy to prevent, following the simple rules of hygiene.

Dehydration
Dehydration - is not enough fluid in the body, which affects its performance. The most dangerous and often there is dehydration (doctors have said dehydration) in the summer. When traveling in countries with a hot climate must be constantly aware of the possibility of developing this condition.

Vomiting
Vomiting usually the result of infection of the gastrointestinal tract. In rare cases, it is associated with a bacterial infection, which is necessary to eliminate the medication. However, in most cases it extends itself for a few days. But vomiting can also occur in other diseases and conditions, some of which require medical intervention.

Shock
Shock - this is a serious condition, when the cardiovascular system is unable to cope with the blood supply of the body, usually associated with low blood pressure and damage to cells or tissue.

Convulsions
Convulsions can vary from mild to severe. With a weak attack of minimal symptoms manifestations, while a severe attack can cause loss of consciousness and sudden and acute muscle spasm caused by the electrical activity of the brain.
