What distinguishes the blood from the veins of blood from a finger? What do the numbers in a sheet with the results of the analysis?

Blood alive to the slightest change in health. That's why the first study that assigns a doctor - a general analysis of blood.

The procedure is simple: disposable needle, lancet on the finger pad makes easy 2-mm puncture, there also appears a drop of blood. If it is not enough, the nurse slightly squeezed finger and pick up the blood in the pipette. A more cunning or import the machine, or a keen eye of an experienced physician, laboratory calculate how much you have what.
