Alcoholism - a disease that occurs as a result of addiction drink alcohol frequently and in excessive quantities. Alcoholics become addicted to alcohol.

Autism
Autism - a violation of psychological development, characterized by disorders of speech and motor skills, activities and stereotyped behaviors that lead to violations of social interaction. Autism may be a result of the disintegration processes of brain development before birth. Autism is strongly influenced by the earlier development of the child, and the consequences of this influence is usually reflected in the life of man.

Bulimia
People suffering from bulimia have a tendency to react to stressful situations, nervously consuming large amounts of food. The triggering factor may have different negative emotions, such as a failure, a failure in anything, exclusion, loneliness, anger, or vice versa, positive, for example, a promotion, a celebration of something or the prospect of a new relationship with the opposite sex.

Depression
Depression - a serious psychological disorder, which often suffer from not only the patient but also his family. Most often, depression develops in the presence of stress or a traumatic situation for a long time. Often depressive disorders are hidden under the guise of a bad mood, character and features can appear at any age and in representatives of all social groups.

How to deal with stress?
Constant stress can have a negative impact on your physical and mental health. Negative stress affects the immune system, causing headaches, fatigue, lethargy, apathy, mood variability, offer you some tips on how to reduce the negative effects of stress.

Eating disorders
Anorexia nervosa - a disorder in which there is pathological fear of getting fat, a voluntary refusal of food and severe weight loss. This disorder is most often in adolescent girls and young women, and is often associated with the fashion for unhealthy thinness and popularity in the society of the image is very thin, almost emaciated girl.

Increased anxiety
Anxiety and worry are integral components of a normal life. Most people feel anxious when faced with danger or any unfamiliar situation. Exam, interview, sporting event or an important meeting in the rate causes a feeling of unease and anxiety.

Chronic Fatigue Syndrome
Fatigue is an overwhelming feeling of tiredness and weakness. All one way or another have experienced such a state in everyday life after a hard, hard work and lack of sleep. Usually, fatigue goes away after a good, proper rest and sleep. If symptoms persist, it means that your body wants to know that he is sick.

Stress
Stress - non-specific reaction to the action of extreme factors, some difficult to solve, or threatening situation. During stress the body produces the hormone adrenaline, which is the main function - to force the body to survive. Stress is a normal part of human life and is necessary in certain quantities.

Schizophrenia
Schizophrenia - this is one of the most common mental illness, characterized by incremental changes in perception, thinking, social activity, speech, feelings and motivations. Most often the disease begins between the ages of 15 to 25 years and has a progressive course.

Epilepsy
Epilepsy - is one of the most common diseases of the nervous system, characterized by recurrent seizures with respect to stereotyping. The incidence of epilepsy is an average of 5-10%.
