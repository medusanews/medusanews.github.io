LOW-DOSE COMBINED OK

Very effective drugs - when used correctly, constant probability of getting pregnant is only 1 in 1000.

The structure of COCs include estrogen and progesterone - the two natural female hormone. A typical package contains 21 tablets, after the end of the cycle to take a break for a week. There are packages and 28 tablets, but 7 of them "dummy" of a different color - the so-called reminiscent of pills that help keep to the rhythm. After OK to be taken every day, preferably at the same time. If you missed taking one tablet, the case can be corrected by taking two pieces the next day. But if you missed two or more days during the week is to insure, for example a condom.

How to act?

First, pills suppress ovulation, preventing the release of an egg from the ovary. Secondly, the thickened mucus, making it difficult for the penetration of sperm into the uterus.

Pros

The method is reliable and thus completely reversible: can conceive after 2 weeks - several months after discontinuation of OK (depending on individual factors).
Suitable pills for women of any age, give birth and childless.
Reduce the risk of some forms of cancer (especially endometrial and ovarian cancer), inflammatory diseases of the pelvic organs, benign tumors of the mammary glands, reducing the risk of ectopic pregnancy. Prevent the development of iron deficiency anemia.
Do not require interruption of reception: they can drink as long as you wish.
Orders the menstrual period: They are not only regular, but less abundant and less painful.

Cons

Some women in the early months may have intermenstrual discharge, mild nausea, mild headache or dizziness, upset stomach, may be a slight increase in weight. This usually occurs when the rhythm gets off the drug.
Not suitable tablet nursing mothers because they can cause a decrease in the amount of breast milk or deterioration of its quality.
Do not take them if you have high blood pressure, migraine, ischemic heart disease, diabetes, complicated by vascular disease, and pregnancy is suspected. Combined OK contraindicated ladies who have or have had breast cancer, stroke.

Heavy smokers over age 35 tablets are contraindicated because of the risk of cardiovascular disease.

At first, cause mood swings or decreased sex drive, usually after 3 months of regular use it passes.
Does not protect against sexually transmitted infections (STIs).

How to act?

The effect of these pills follow the natural processes of the female body during breast-feeding. The contraceptive effect is based on thickening the mucus at the entrance of the cervix.

About half of women minipill suppress ovulation.

Pros

Does not affect the quantity and quality of breast milk.
