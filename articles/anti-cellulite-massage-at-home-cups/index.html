The principles and techniques of anti-cellulite self-massage

The basic principles of anti-cellulite self-massage is quite simple – having mastered them, you can always maintain your skin in perfect condition, without resorting to expensive beauty salons.

Cellulite self-massage should be carried out energetic, but lightly. Common misconception of many women is that, ostensibly, to use as much physical strength. If you overdo and you will be too hard to knead and squeeze the skin, you will make a reverse effect – the deterioration of the situation and the weakening of lymph flow. There are cases where the use of unnecessary force due to the effect of cellulite noticeably intensified.

To avoid compromising the lymph flow is prohibited to massage in the groin, thigh dimples, inner upper thighs and the area around the pubic area.

The massage should be required from the bottom up.

For best results, before a session of self-massage, heat the good body with exercise or a warm shower. Hands should also not be cold – warm them by rubbing your palms against each other.

In order to achieve rapid results cellulite self-massage is desirable to carry out daily, ideally twice a day – morning and evening for 15-30 minutes. The first tangible results in the fight against cellulite, with due diligence, typically appear after 1-2 months.

Key anti-cellulite self-massage techniques of hand

In the exercise of self-massage hand-applied 3 main reception – rubbing, squeezing and pat.

Grinding – is carried out in a circular motion (clockwise) of the entire surface of the palm. In the thighs rubbing should be done in a circular motion, and on his feet – from the bottom up.

Pressing – at this reception to grab with both hands as much as possible the skin and knead “the dough”, while avoiding compression of the skin. Pushing effectively improves the tone flabby muscles.

Pat – the most effective massage techniques, carried out or the whole hand, or knuckles, clenched in a fist. In this case, applied a series of rhythmic strokes of varying intensity. Pat can be done to rhythmic music, producing them in time.
