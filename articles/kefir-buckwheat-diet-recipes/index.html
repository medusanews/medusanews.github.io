The essence of buckwheat diet

The essence of buckwheat diet is that the body is not getting out of the usual fast food carbohydrates, forced to earn them, splitting the internal fat.

Therefore, a person begins to lose weight quickly. Buckwheat diet belongs to the category of low-carb protein mono diet and is designed for no more than 7-14 days, depending on the “severity” of compliance. Longer terms are undesirable and can have negative consequences as buckwheat, for all its usefulness and uniqueness are not able to supply the body with all the necessary.

To stringent restrictions in the diet did not cause the body shock, nutritionists advise, before you get on a long diet, try a fasting day on buckwheat and look at your health. In addition, doctors warn that the buckwheat diet, like the popular Japanese now, is not for everyone, it is not recommended for people with diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, hypertension, diabetes, and pregnant and lactating mothers. No matter how you were pleased with the results achieved, it is not recommended to re-sit her sooner than 1-1.5 months after the end of the previous and provided adequate nutrition, not to cause irreparable harm to the body.
