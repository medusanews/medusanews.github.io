Cellulite. Causes, Symptoms, Treatments

Jan 3rd, 2016 3:43 pm

Cellulite in medicine refers to inflammation of subcutaneous fat.

But really popular word "cellulite" was when he began to communicate with the perennial problem, which is so concerned about the people who want to have a beautiful body. Known to most people cellulite - it changed the appearance of skin with subcutaneous fat. Cellulite usually occur on the thighs, buttocks and stomach, at least - in the armpits, chest, upper arms.

Cellulite - is not a disease, but an esthetic problem, which is inherent in the female constitution. He varying severity occurs in about 80% of women aged over 20 years. A common misconception - is that cellulite is only complete. Most characteristic changes occur in women of normal weight and even located on the lower limit of normal body mass index.

The mechanism of formation of cellulite

Under the influence of certain factors (heredity, diet, hormonal factors, physical inactivity, the deterioration of the microcirculation and others.) In the fat cells increases the deposition of fat and reducing its metabolic degradation. As a result, fat cells subcutaneous tissue uneven increase in size. Strong long connective partitions between them tightened and pressed into the part of the fat cells, and on the sides of the strands of fat portions protrude more, leading to even more uneven skin surface.

Causes and risk factors of cellulite

Overweight - an increase in body fat in itself leads to increased levels of estrogen in the blood, which exacerbates the appearance of cellulite.

Hormones: growth of fat cells is dependent on the level of the female hormone estrogen, so women with a higher concentration of estrogen in the blood more prone to the risk of the appearance of cellulite. This again indicates that the light level of cellulite it is almost normal, but its first manifestations should be a signal to work on yourself. In the case of predisposition to cellulite you need to keep yourself in good shape, so as not to aggravate the appearance of cellulite.

Use of hormonal contraceptives - an artificial change in the blood levels of sex hormones also affects the rate of growth of fat cells.

Poor circulation and lack of exercise - are interrelated. Circulatory disturbance occurs when a long stay in one position, low muscle tone and lack of physical activity, wearing tight underwear, stockings, a long walk in high heels.

Heredity - often cellulite occurs in those who have a genetic predisposition to develop it. So if someone in your immediate family has any degree of cellulite, you need to start it early prevention. The best prevention of cellulite - proper nutrition and physical activity.

The symptoms of cellulitis

The skin is covered with small pits and often reminiscent of orange peel. Cellulite is different depending on the severity. In mild cases it is visible only when the skin is trapped in the fold. In more severe cases, it gives the skin an uneven appearance with dimples and pits. Symptoms of cellulite worsens with age as the skin loses its elasticity.

Cellulite is not a serious medical problem, and compulsory treatment is not necessary. Although if you believe in complete aesthetic disaster on your thighs or buttocks, you can seek help from a doctor or dermatologist plastic surgeon. The skilled artisan will prompt the most effective ways to solve the problem and give an approximate forecast of the results of different methods of treatment for you.

Cellulite Treatment

Many advertised ways to combat cellulite - creams, massage devices - promise significant results. But, unfortunately, most of these methods are "not fulfill" promises. The most effective methods to combat cellulite:

  • Weight loss - a healthy diet and regular exercise - at the moment it is the most effective and safe way to treat cellulite. Strengthening the muscles of the legs, buttocks, arms, abdominals and obliques, and getting rid of extra kilos can significantly improve the landscape and appearance of the skin. Do not expect immediate results from these methods, and the more you can not throw a healthy lifestyle, a week later if you did not notice the changes. On strengthening the muscles it takes time more than one week. In addition, cellulite is sometimes tends to diminish some time after returning to a normal weight. Although the results are usually still visible after a few weeks of starting treatment. In addition, proper low-calorie food coupled with exercise - it is the only method that does not threaten your health and life, and just goes in his favor.

  • Operation with a laser - is currently considered the most promising surgical method. There are several methods of laser treatment. One method involves a combination of tissue massage, radiofrequency exposure to fatty tissue and infrared radiation. Another method is to deliver into the subcutaneous tissue diode laser energy. The third technique uses high-frequency effects on the deep and superficial layers of the subcutaneous tissue. All varieties of laser treatment to produce results post treatment. The result, however, unstable, and the average remains about 6 months. Laser treatment is considered minimally invasive (causing minimum damage to the tissues of the body) and produces a purposeful destruction of fat cells with accompanying skin tightening.

  • Liposuction - the name of the operation for a long time at the hearing. During surgery, the surgeon places through small incisions under the skin of a narrow tube through which oil is pumped out and destroyed. But liposuction does not eliminate cellulite, because it comes from the superficial layers of the subcutaneous tissue and fat is removed from the deeper layers. Therefore, liposuction can sometimes even worsen the appearance of cellulite.

Local treatment - cream with a variety of active extracts, massage. The most successful proven extracts of grapefruit. Although the effect of it is very different and poorly defined. Also, according to recent studies, creams containing 0.3% retinol shown to be effective against cellulite after 6 months of use. But these resources, however, and massage can be used only as an adjunct to a healthy diet and physical activity.

Remember that surgical treatments always carry a certain risk (reaction to anesthesia, extensive tissue damage), so before you use them, it is better to try the safest way: strengthening of muscles and weight loss. If you once will acquire the habit of a healthy and active lifestyle, it will bring you in addition to the disappearance or reduction of cellulite and many other useful "bonus". So try to choose the most convenient method to combat cellulite.
