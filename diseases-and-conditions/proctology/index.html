Rectal abscess
Paraprocts - purulent inflammation of the tissues surrounding the rectum. Distinguish acute (first emerged), and chronic abscess (developed as a result of inadvertent or improper opening (treatment) of acute paraproctitis).

Hemorrhoids
Hemorrhoids - hemorrhoid is varicose veins of the rectum, accompanied by symptoms such as discomfort and pain, bleeding, prolapsed hemorrhoids, etc. Hemorrhoids is one of the most common diseases of all civilized countries.

Acute intestinal obstruction
Acute intestinal obstruction - this is a life-threatening pathological condition which is characterized by passing the contents against gastrointestinal tract towards the stomach to the anus and is usually a complication of various diseases. In the absence of timely treatment the prognosis of bowel obstruction extremely poor.

Colon polyps
Colon polyps - benign tumor formation growing out of the intestinal wall into the lumen. Any polyp should be considered as a precancerous condition. However, long-lived polyp can be transformed into a malignant tumor - colorectal cancer. Carriers of these tumors can be both children and adults.

Proctitis
Proctitis - inflammation of the lining of the rectum. Proctitis may contribute to the development of hypothermia, diseases of the rectum and the adjacent organs - hemorrhoids, anal fissure, abscess, abscess Douglas space, prostatitis, cystitis, vulvovaginitis, etc. If untreated, can cause such complications as a narrowing of the rectum.
