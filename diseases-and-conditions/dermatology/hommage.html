Hommage

Sep 22nd, 2013

The name comes from the French word “gomme” - “eraser”. Indicates a special cream, and the procedure to remove the top layer of the skin.

Hommage - is the same peeling or scrub, but gentle, for sensitive, aging or acne prone skin. Exfoliation cream does not contain any mechanical parts that act like sandpaper and gently dissolves and removes dead skin cells, moisturizes and tones the skin.

Hommage put on the face and after 10-15 minutes, when it dries, roll up in a circular motion along with the top layer of the epidermis, the skin while holding the other hand, that it will not stretch. If there are pustules on the face, the cream is better just rinse with water.

Sometimes special hommage to men - it is recommended to apply once or twice a week before shaving to facilitate this is not the most pleasant procedure. Exfoliating microbeads gently remove dead skin cells and extracts of ginseng, aloe, licorice and thermal water restores skin’s natural defenses.

Despite the softness Hommage, it’s still peeling. It is not worth doing on vacation, when you many hours a day spend in the air, the sun, in the salty sea breeze.
