Dermatology

Sep 22nd, 2013

Acne
Acne occur when hair follicles in the skin become clogged due to secretions of the sebaceous glands and dead cells of the skin, and often affect the face, back, chest and arms. The tendency to exacerbate acne can be inherited.

Atopic dermatitis
Atopic dermatitis (it’s also called atopic dermatitis) is a chronic inflammatory disease of the skin allergic nature, the main symptom is severe itching. Predisposition to diffuse neurodermatitis, and other atopic diseases (bronchial asthma, pollinosis, allergic rhinitis) genetically transmitted.

Care for problem skin
Problem skin - skin that is prone to the appearance of acne (acne vulgaris). In severe acne should consult a dermatologist, but with mild to moderate severity is quite possible to try to cope on their own. The main thing - regularly and properly care for the skin. Proper skin care - is half the victory over rashes, irritation, scaling and sallow complexion. Meticulous care of the skin to help stop new lesions, reduce treatment time, reduce the need for drugs and cosmetic procedures, will cope with irritation and flaking and improve the complexion.

Caring for aging skin
If the first signs of skin aging appear about 35 years, after 45 signs of aging become more pronounced deep wrinkles appear, somewhat “blurred” contour face, the skin becomes very dry and dull. Such skin, women over the age of 45 years, referred to as aging. To make a woman feel young and attractive at that age, her skin requires thorough daily care.

Caring for mature skin
 Mature commonly referred to as the skin in women aged 35-45 years. In this age the skin starts to decrease production of collagen - a special protein responsible for its elasticity. The skin becomes dry and dull, and shows the shallow and deep wrinkles. Particularly rapid areas around the eyes and mouth, where the skin is thin and delicate, and neck. Even normal skin, is never delivered to the problems of their happy owner after 35 years begins to require special care.

Corns and calluses chronic
Corns - an area of hardened, chapped skin, which was formed as a result of continuous or repeated friction and pressure, most often on the hands and feet. These lesions are harmless and usually painless. Corn skin - is yellowish education, usually at the tips of the toes, where they are most strongly rub against the shoe. If the friction continues, corn may become red, inflamed and begin to cause pain. Corns are usually held as a result of self-medication, and do not deliver much trouble.

Dandruff
Dandruff - this is a common, harmless, rather cosmetic than a medical problem that often occurs after a stressful and can cause thinning hair. The cause of dandruff is a sharp increase in the number and activity of the fungus Pityrosporum ovale.

Demodicosis
Demodicosis - a skin disease that causes a parasitic mite glands acne. Demodicosis most often seen in folliculitis (inflammation of the hair follicles of the skin). The disease is chronic, often occurs in people with weakened immune or metabolic disorders. Diagnosis and treatment of this disease has been dermatologist.

Dermatitis
Dermatitis - a syndrome which is a skin inflammatory response to various stimuli. Distinguish contact dermatitis and drug reaction. Contact dermatitis occur when the direct effects of the stimulus on the skin, toksidermii foreign substances penetrate into the internal environment of the body, causing a series of changes, one manifestation of which is dermatitis. There are also seborrheic dermatitis (dandruff, acne), atopic dermatitis (allergic) dermatitis herpetiformis, eczema.

Diseases of the skin
Leather - very large organ in the human body. Of the skin, nails and hair often reflects the overall condition of the body and are an important part of a person’s appearance. With some of the problems of the skin, hair and nails can manage on their own at home. In many cases, you may need the help of a dermatologist or allergist.

Fillers
Fillers - are materials that beauticians used to fill wrinkles, lip augmentation, and creation of fabrics.

Foot mycosis
Before fungal foot problems were so widespread that few could avoid infection. Fortunately, effective medications are available. But so far, athlete’s foot is the most common fungal disease of the skin. The most commonly affected folds of skin between the toes and the surface of the foot (especially the soles), but in rare cases may even be involved hands.

Furuncles
Furuncles - it’s painful, inflamed, pus-filled cavity in the skin, most often they occur in areas where there is a scalp and where the trenie. Frequent furuncles (furunculosis) may indicate the presence of diabetes.

Hair loss
Hair loss can be caused by various diseases, hormonal imbalances, head injury or damage to the scalp, stress or taking certain medications. Often the hair begins to grow again in eliminating the causes loss. Alopecia (alopecia) pattern baldness (alopecia) - a disease in which the lost hair in place remain smooth round spots.

Hair Removal
Remove from sight unwanted vegetation is not easy. Shaving, plucking, and bioepilyatsiya (with sugar syrup, resin or wax) bring desired, but, alas, short-term effect. It deletes only the visible part of the hair, and the hair follicle remains intact. Remove hair permanently help electric or light (photo-and laser) hair removal. But let us all methods of hair removal more.

Hommage
The name comes from the French word “homme” - “eraser”. Indicates a special cream, and the procedure to remove the top layer of the skin.

Impetigo
Impetigo - a disease characterized by the formation of surface bubble-pustular rash. The disease is very contagious. Impetigo often becomes the scourge of children’s groups, most often it occurs in small teams, nursery children. Impetigo is dangerous because it can produce complications in the internal organs, so that any outbreak of the disease is thoroughly investigated, and children prolechivayutsya.

Itchy skin
Itchy skin - one of the most distressing symptoms that a person can experience. Fortunately, in most cases the competent doctor is able to determine the cause of the itching and eliminate it.

Keloids
Keloids - a special type of scarring, penetrating into the surrounding normal tissue. They remain for a lifetime, often creating a significant cosmetic and functional defects. Disfiguring scars, scars that limit even small movements can cause serious problems in patients, both in business and in personal life.

Male pattern baldness
Of male pattern baldness (doctors say “androgenetic alopecia”) is due to hereditary reasons and hormonal state of the body. Though to be more precise, this hair loss is mainly observed in men, but is inherited through the maternal. Women have less severe form of this type of baldness and usually develops later in life.

Mesotherapy
Even the best creams are effective only on the skin surface. Reach the deep layer and rejuvenate at the cellular level enable the injection of beauty - Mesotherapy.

Miliaria
Miliaria is associated with intense sweating and is most common in infants and obese people. Sudamen leads to the appearance of clear, shiny bubbles filled with fluid, which usually do dry quickly without any treatment.

Molluscum contagiosum
Molluscum contagiosum - a viral disease of the skin, which manifests itself in the form of specific rash papules with umbilicated. Molluscum contagiosum may suffer both children and adults. The disease often occurs in people with weakened immune systems, such as in HIV-infected patients.

Peeling
The age-old women’s desire for beauty and eternal youth today is responsible peeling. This method allows you to throw off the old skin, and with it, get rid of the annoying drawbacks: wrinkles and age spots.

Pink versicolor
Pink versicolor - is an acute dermatitis, which is part of a group of inflammatory skin diseases of unknown origin. In most cases, pink lichen is safe and resolves on its own in 6-12 weeks. However, it should be remembered that when the pink shingles rash similar to the manifestations of secondary syphilis, so the diagnosis is necessary to conduct further investigation.

Psoriasis
Psoriasis - is a skin disease in which there are red scaly patches covered with silvery scales that cause itching. Psoriasis usually occur in men aged 20 to 30 years and often hereditary.

Thermage
This cosmetic procedure is often referred to as “non-surgical tightening” and even said that now the plastic does not need to be.

The steps of facial skin care
There is a certain set of cosmetics that every woman should have, as well as a certain sequence in which they are applied. Knowledge of this sequence will allow you to have in your arsenal of tools needed daily care and always look well-groomed and beautiful.

Treatment for severe acne
In severe acne self-treatment is not enough - it is necessary to consult a dermatologist, preferably a specialist in acne. Acne can be severe, chronic relapsing disease that causes significant psychological experiences and complexes. But do not despair: with any form of acne, even the most severe and prolonged, can be overcome. Of course, the treatment of severe acne, like any chronic disease, will take time and require patience and careful adherence to all the doctor’s recommendations. But a significant improvement and even complete recovery is possible and attainable.

Treatment of psoriasis
Psoriasis is one of the diseases that have a strong impact on the social adaptation of people. It is therefore the treatment of psoriasis is aimed at improving the skin not only the process but also the quality of life of the patient. It should be understood, however, that because the causes of psoriasis are not known to get rid of psoriasis forever impossible, but it can be achieved sustained remission (weakening or complete disappearance of symptoms).

Trichomycosis
Diseases that occur in lesions of hair mushrooms, called trichomycosis. The main pathogens are fungi of the genus trichomycosis Microsporium (causes the disease known as ringworm) and Trichophyton. The sources of infection are sick people, homeless animals (mostly cats) or contaminated with spores of fungi household items (tools in the barber shop, hats, seat backs in public places, etc. etc.). Mycosis of the scalp are more common in children.

Vitiligo
Vitiligo - occurs at any age and manifested by the appearance of white spots on the skin. The disease is usually chronic, mentally ill and depressing causing discomfort. Vitiligo - not just unpleasant cosmetic defect, but also more than a visual reminder of the serious violations of the body.

Warts
Warts - are benign growths on the skin or mucous membranes. Warts that appear on the soles of the feet are called plantar. They often disappear on their own, but sometimes it can remain in place for several years, or occasionally disappear and reappear.
